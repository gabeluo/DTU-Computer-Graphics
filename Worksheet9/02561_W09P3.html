Compare projection shadows to shadow mapping by listing advantages and disadvantages of the two techniques.

Projection Shadows

Pros:
Quick and easy to implement

Cons:
Doesn't show any self-shadowing
More inefficient with multiple objects as each object needs to have its own shadow drawn as a separate object
Difficult to implement with more complex shapes (consider how adding alpha blending with a teapot will not work)

Shadow Mapping

Pros:
Allows for self-shadowing (consider the little nub at the top of the teapot)
Doesn't require clipping of the shadow

Cons:
Quality is determined by the resolution of the shadow map
Aliasing problems (staircase effect at the edge of the shadows)
Can be harder to implement as it takes time to find the proper projection and view matrices for the light source
Unwanted effects or improper shadows can occur if the shadow map precision isn't very high
